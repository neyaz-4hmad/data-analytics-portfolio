# CS1 — Retail Sales Analysis

## Business Problem
A retail chain wants to understand which product categories, regions, and time periods are driving revenue — and where performance is lagging — to prioritise stock and marketing spend.

## Dataset
- Transactions with product category, region, date, quantity, and revenue

## Key Findings
- Identified top-performing and underperforming product categories by revenue contribution
- Seasonal trends revealed peak and off-peak months for inventory planning
- Regional breakdown highlighted which stores need support vs. which can be scaled

## Recommendations
- Increase stock for top categories ahead of peak months
- Review pricing or discontinue bottom 20% SKUs
- Replicate best-performing regional strategies across lagging stores

## Techniques Used
- `groupby` + `agg` for category and regional summaries
- `matplotlib` bar charts for revenue comparison
- Date-based filtering for trend analysis
